About Me

I'm Sreelakshmi Gangadharan, an aspiring web developer with 2 years' experience as a project associate for Steer. I come from Kerala,India and currently live in United Arab Emirates.

So, I'll tell you how this all got started.

Coding was never my thing until I decided to have a career change from my project associate role. As a relative beginner, I had trouble getting into the swing of coding again.

I tried to learn to code by watching videos on the internet, but it didn't work. So I had to get my hands dirty and actually write some code. And now I just have to remember not to get too excited when the code actually works!

Currently, I spend most of my time learning, designing, prototyping, and coding. Outside of my laptop, I may be doing some scribbling or watching movies — or perhaps both. Sky-obsessed, I often spend hours gazing at the hues and the stars.
